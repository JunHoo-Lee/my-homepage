% Research Interests
\section{Research Interests}
My research aims to bridge the gap between \textbf{optimization theory} and \textbf{modern generative AI}. Instead of merely scaling up models, I investigate the training dynamics of overparameterized networks and design inductive biases (such as geometric constraints or explicit filtering) to ensure robust in-distribution learning. Currently, I am exploring the fundamental principles of \textbf{Diffusion Models} and \textbf{LLMs} to make them more efficient, explainable, and controllable.
